Designed on 5/25/25

As a cost-cutting measure pulled by VW engineers, they placed 5 seperate touch capacitive buttons on a single strip of plastic, making it impossible to know where you are clicking without looking down. I designed a cover that clips on and adds ridges, helping guide your finger to where you want it as you are barrelling down the highway.
